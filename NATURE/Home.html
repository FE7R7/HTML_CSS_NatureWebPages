Take Care of Nature, to be able to live and enjoy it...

How Important?

It doesn't matter if you live in a big city, on the coast or in the countryside, nature is essential for your well-being! Nature provides human beings with everything from basic elements for life (water, food, energy, clean air) to opportunities for recreation, inspiration and psychological and spiritual benefits. In scientific jargon, these are ecosystem services, also known more recently as “nature's contributions to people”

So, let's watch our actions because this is about RESPECT and being AWARE of our responsibilities to the planet...
